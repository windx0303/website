Writing

In Taiwan, I'm known as "風神" (Feng-Shen) who writes fictions, short stories, essays, plays, and occasionally poems in Mandarin.

I started writing since elementary school. When I was 17, I managed to collect some money to self-publish my first book. Later, I was lucky enough to be accetped as a double-major student by the Chinese Literature department of National Taiwan Univeristy and eventaully obtained my B.A. there. In 2010, I won the "Award of Mystery Writers of Taiwan (台灣推理作家協會徵文獎)".

I'm still writing (something not academic papers) today. You can find some of my latest pieces on my blog, windx.cc (風神之舞). I like Haruki Murakami (村上春樹), Kazufumi Shiraishi (白石一文), and the Taiwanese writer 張惠菁.

Two writing/art projects I did recently were "One Hundred Books (書一百)" and "The First Day of the Year (正月初一頭一天)".
